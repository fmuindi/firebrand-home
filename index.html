Who We Are

A fire that doesn't fade

Firebrand Nation is a Spirit-led movement born out of a conviction that God is raising up a generation that will not be silenced — a generation that carries the fire of revival into every sphere of society.

From the streets of Nairobi to nations across Africa and beyond, we preach the Gospel with power, plant seeds of transformation, and equip ordinary people for extraordinary Kingdom impact.

Learn about us
Outreach

Reaching the unreached. Restoring the broken.

Our outreach arm goes where hope is scarce. From feeding programs and medical camps to prison ministry and youth empowerment — we believe the Gospel must be seen as much as it is heard.

Every crusade. Every community visit. Every conversation on the street. Heaven touching earth.
